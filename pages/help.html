The Purpose of the Coca-Cola e-Learning tool is to provide you with an easy-to-use, informative and interactive method for upgrading your knowledge.
Each chapter includes two parts:

  • a. A Q&A section to help you refresh your knowledge and speed up your progress. You may go through the Q&A section before or after the quiz.
  • b. A short quiz to test what you have learnt and what you remember about our products

All quizzes are in the form of multiple-choice and there is only one correct answer to every question.
After completing each question, press “CONTINUE” to get to the next question.

The Quiz will be complete, only if you answer all of the questions correctly. Otherwise, you will be kindly asked to retry the quiz from the beginning.

Once you complete ALL 4 QUIZZES in all 4 chapters – the entire Coca-Cola e-Learning Tool will be marked as completed.
We wish you a fun learning experience!
