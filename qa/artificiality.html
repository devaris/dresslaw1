Artificiality

1. How can something 'artificial' be good for me to eat or drink?

“Artificial” does not necessarily mean “bad” and “natural” does not necessarily mean “good”!

2. What are food additives?

Additives are substances that are added in foodstuff to perform a specific technological function.

3. What does the 'E' in front of some Coca-Cola ingredients mean?

E-numbers are a systematic way of “coding” different food additives in the European Union (EU).

4. How many additives does Coca-Cola have?

2

5. How can it be that Coca-Cola has no added preservatives?

The unique combination of ingredients is responsible for the stability of Coca-Cola.

6. Are food additives safe?

All authorized food additives, including the ones in our products, are safe.

7. I have heard that some E numbers are more dangerous than others.

There is NO SUCH THING as good or bad, safe or dangerous E numbers. They are all safe when used at levels specified by authorities.
