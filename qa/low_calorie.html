Low calorie sweeteners

1. What are low / no calorie sweeteners?

Food additives with intense sweet taste that provide few or no calories.

2. Are low / no calorie sweeteners safe?

Yes, LNCS as all food additives are approved ONLY after undergoing a strict safety evaluation by food safety authorities.

3. Can LNCS really help with weight management?

Yes, they can help manage weight if used consistently to reduce calorie intake as part of an overall sensible, balanced diet, combined with regular physical activity.

4. Are low / no calorie sweeteners safe for all populations?

Yes, LNCS are safe to be consumed by all populations. Aspartame cannot be consumed by people suffering from the rare genetic disorder PKU (Phenylketonuria).

5. So, if LNCSS Are safe, why do we keep on reading so many negatives about them?

Unfounded claims that something is unsafe travels faster and is deemed more interesting to publish. Health authorities all over the world assure us that LNCS are safe.

6. Is aspartame safe?

Yes. Hundreds of studies have been conducted to support the safety of aspartame. It is one of the most thoroughly researched and studied food ingredients in use today.
